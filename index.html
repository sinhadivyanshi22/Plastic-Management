Three pillars of our organization.

Reduce

This phase involves activities undertaken to reduce plastic use by opting for reusable alternatives like stainless steel water bottles, cloth shopping bags, and glass containers. Avoid single-use plastics such as straws, utensils, and plastic wrap. Choose products with minimal or eco-friendly packaging. Small changes in daily habits can significantly decrease plastic consumption and waste.

Recycle

This phase encompasses the recycling of plastic items like bottles, containers, and packaging according to local guidelines. Rinse containers to remove food residue before recycling. Use designated recycling bins for sorting materials properly. Support businesses and initiatives that promote plastic recycling. Recycling conserves resources and reduces environmental impact.

Reuse

This phase focuses on reusing items like water bottles, shopping bags, and food containers instead of using disposable ones. Repurpose old containers for storage or crafts. Borrow or share items like tools or books to reduce consumption. Embrace a circular economy by giving new life to existing products. Reusing reduces waste and conserves resources.
